history

Though not of the age old, it is historical to be talked of as an event springing from the times prior to the ones we now dwell in. The whole idea was conceived into the minds of the pioneers as a simple mathematical refreshment and stimulant, in form of a trivial internal quiz to mark the end of Maths year 2019. As a result, the last week of November, 2019 saw the Mathematicians of the band cruise through an oral problems based contest session in which a vigorous and spirited problem solving activity ensued, as new Mathematical ideas and elation permeated the psyches of the participants. It was the first of its kind at the time. This oral phase was then followed by a written presentation in the Arvo. The atmosphere of the day’s aftermath of the quiz then saw the Mathematicians resolve to be holding the kind of event annually. Hitherto, the Mathletics Contest still unfolds the glamour of Mathematics, year in, year out. After the Maths attack of the day(s), the club then crowns the distinguished Mathletes as well as an overall Maths cracker, the Maths King of the year.
