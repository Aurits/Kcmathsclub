About The MathematicsContest


The Mathletics Contest is an internal and annual sensational Maths competition of the Kiira College Butiki Mathematics Club, that brings together artistic and endowed lads of the hill to showcase their pre-eminent proficiency in Mathematics covering the four prominent areas of the IMO; number theory, combinatorics, geometry and algebra.


The nature of the contest

The head panel of the contest accepts Mathletes below the age 20 and below, to take part in the contest. The contestants are divide into three categories namely Junior (Juvenile) category, Intermediate category and Senior category.

Junior (Juvenile) category

This level consists of Mathletes preferably below the age of 15. However, according to the countryâs curriculum and school setting, a contestant that is 15 or above, but in Senior Two and below is accepted to take part under this category. The top Mathlete of this level is then chosen to compete in the Maths King Challenge platform.

Intermediate catergory

The intermediate category allows in contestants below the age of 17, though a Mathlete that is 17 or above who is in Senior 4 and below is allowed to compete in the Mathletics contest at the Intermediate platform. Likewise, the top Mathlete at the Intermediate level is also selected to go and compete at the Maths King Challenge platform.

Senior catergory

The oral part consists of short mind seizing and stimulating oral questions which are answered in time frame of a minute. The written phase consists of two sections A and B. In section A are 10 compulsory short answer stimulating problems from all the four areas of algebra, combinatorics, geometry and number theory in almost equal proportions. Each problem weighs exactly 5 points or marks. Section B has got exactly 5 high order mathematical problems each weighing 10 points or marks each, still from the four areas above in almost equal proportions. Problems vary according to category and therefore the level of their demands and appeal is proportional to the Mathletesâ levels
